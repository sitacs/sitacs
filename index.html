About US

Department of Computer Science Engineering
[B.E- CSE]
proudly presents SITACS'23 with Loads of Events and Tons of Gifts for the Student Community

The goal of this symposium is to bring together engineering students to focus on understanding the latest and emerging trends in Technology, and establishing new group efforts in these areas. Hence, we would like to cordially invite all the students to actively participate in all the events which would benefit them in their academics and co-curricular activities.
